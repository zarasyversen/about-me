About me

Background

I am originally from Sweden but moved to Brighton in 2015. I completed a web design diploma from Hove College in February 2016 and spent 1 year working on my skills and building my portfolio in my spare time. During this year, I was a regular attendee of codebar Brighton, which is an amazing non-profit initiative that helps underrepresented get in to tech. Besides working on my portfolio, I met loads of passionate developers around Brighton. Thanks to the connections I made, I landed my first web developer role in June 2017.

I am currently a junior front-end developer at Gene Commerce. Gene is a brilliant Magento agency with some really exciting clients.

Free time

I still spend most of my Tuesday evenings at codebar, that I now co-organise with 3 other lovely women and I regularly coach beginners in HTML/CSS. I love attending other tech events around Brighton like SheSays.
